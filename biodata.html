WHO ARE YOU?!? YOU MUST KNOW THEM

Din Djarin

known as The Mandalorian or Mando, is a stoic bounty hunter from a secretive Mandalorian sect, bound by a strict creed of honor and never removing his helmet. Voiced and portrayed by Pedro Pascal, he protects the Force-sensitive child Grogu across the galaxy, evolving from lone wolf to reluctant father figure in a post-Empire era. His beskar armor and jetpack make him a symbol of Mandalorian resilience.

Din Grogu

Affectionately called Baby Yoda by fans, is a mysterious 50-year-old infant of Yoda's rare species, gifted with Force abilities like telekinesis. Voiced with coos and cries by various sound designers (no speaking role), he's the heart of the series, forming an unbreakable bond with Din Djarin as his protector, symbolizing innocence and hope in a war-torn galaxy.

Luke Skywalker

The legendary Jedi hero from the original trilogy, returns as a powerful, scarred master seeking to rebuild the Order. Portrayed by a de-aged Mark Hamill via deepfake and motion capture, he mentors Grogu in Season 2, showcasing his lightsaber prowess and internal struggles with the dark side's temptations.

Ahsoka Tano

Anakin Skywalker's former Padawan turned rebel leader, is a wise Togruta warrior wielding white lightsabers. Voiced and portrayed by Rosario Dawson (live-action debut), she hunts Imperial threats and guides Din Djarin in Season 2, embodying independence from Jedi dogma.

Moff Gideon

Cunning Imperial warlord commanding remnant forces, obsessed with Grogu's blood for dark cloning experiments. Portrayed by Giancarlo Esposito with chilling menace, he deploys advanced tech like dark troopers, serving as the series' primary antagonist in Seasons 1-3.

Bo Katan-Kryzee

A fierce Mandalorian noble and rebel leader seeking to reclaim her throne with the Darksaber. Portrayed by Katee Sackhoff (from animated roots), she allies with Din in Seasons 2-3, representing pragmatic politics over rigid tradition.

The Armorer

The Armorer is the enigmatic forge-master of Din's Mandalorian covert, crafting beskar gear and enforcing the creed. Portrayed by Emily Swallow with ritualistic poise, she guides warriors spiritually and practically, blessing foundlings and forging alliances.

Carasynthia Dune

ex-Rebel Shock Trooper turned enforcer for the New Republic, skilled in hand-to-hand combat. Portrayed by Gina Carano with raw intensity, she becomes Din's ally on Nevarro, protecting Grogu and clashing with Imperial holdouts.

Boba Fett
